how it works

Start a lap. Write a note. Watch the shape of your day appear.

Lap timer

Start, stop, and tag what you were doing. Laps stack into a running log with comments and a category for each one.

Timeline view

A horizontal chart of your day — each lap rendered as a block sized to its real duration, side by side on a clock.

Heatmap

A calendar-style view of your activity over weeks and months, so the long view is just as visible as the daily one.

Journal

A running markdown notebook alongside the laps, for the context numbers alone don't capture.

Local-first by design. The app writes to a SQLite file on your own disk. There's no account, no sign-in, and nothing leaves your machine unless you explicitly export it.
